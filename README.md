## The 5-Rung Mastery Ladder

Real change is sequential. You can't fix a higher problem while the foundation is missing — so The Master **diagnoses which rung your problem truly lives on and routes you to the lowest unlearned one first.** Each rung is also a **Taekwondo Tier** and ties to **one book**.

| Tier | Principle | What your child (and you) learn | Belt journey |
|:--:|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Self-Control** | Emotions, peers, and environment *guide* choices — they don't *dictate* them | White → Yellow → Orange |
| **2** | **Self-Awareness** | Know where you are and what you're meant to be doing | Green → Purple → Light Blue |
| **3** | **Strength of Character** | True confidence from knowledge → practice → skill (never arrogance) | Dark Blue → Brown → Red |
| **4** | **"The Soup"** | Results, not reasons — for every problem, find a solution | Deputy Black Belt |
| **5** | **Leadership** | Serve needs over wants; be the calm in the storm | Black Belt |

**Finish all five tiers and earn a real black belt.**

- After a belt's lessons, **submit your form to The Master, pay a one-time $20 review, and test up.** Pass, and the next belt opens.
- Complete the path → **a real, earned black belt** — and your ladder progress advances automatically.
